## 1. No Data Collection

tiny does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data — not locally to any remote server, and not to the Developer or any third party.

The App has no internet permission. It is architecturally incapable of sending any data anywhere.

## 2. Permissions

tiny requests only the permissions required to function as an Android home screen launcher:

- **Query installed apps** — to display your app drawer and pinned apps.
- **Set as default launcher** — to function as your home screen.

No permissions are used to access contacts, location, camera, microphone, files, or any other personal data.

## 3. Local Storage

The App stores your pinned app selection in Android's standard `SharedPreferences` on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is only used to remember which apps you have pinned to your home screen.

## 4. No Internet Access

tiny does not have the `INTERNET` permission and does not make any network requests. There are no ads, no remote analytics, no crash reporting, and no feature flags delivered over the network.

## 5. No Advertisements

The App contains no advertisements of any kind.

## 6. Third-Party Services

tiny does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Because no data is collected from anyone, tiny is safe for users of all ages. It does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 or any other age threshold defined by applicable law.
